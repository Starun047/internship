Gongura Mutton

This dish is made both in Andhra and Telangana. Mostly made in Andhra, it is very simple to make and require a few ingredients only. If the Mutton is cooked well with proportionate Salt and Chilli powder, you get a perfect taste. It is generally made in the same method by all Telugu people. Ingredients differ depending upon the region. I am following Guntur Style Curry here.This is an age old recipe. The dish served in Restaurants is slightly different as they mix Mutton and Sorrel once the order has been placed so the Mutton doesn't absorb the sourness from the leaves and may taste bland.

Veg Biryani

Biryani is a celebratory dish popular in many countries. A traditional biryani has layers of marinated meat and fragrant rice, dum cooked with caramelized onions, spices, herbs and saffron-infused milk. Making a good biryani, especially the traditional method requires time and patience since it involves a series of steps like marination, cooking biryani gravy, parcooking rice, layering and lastly dum cooking.
